Controls:
Up/Down Arrow Keys - Movement
Right/Left Arrow Keys - Rotation
Space Bar - Shoot

Directions: Shoot the jumbled red words to break them apart. Collect the parts in the right order!

Welcome to the trial version of Word Blaster, a literacy game!

Enter Words


Choose how many words to enter using the '+' button, then enter the words you would like to play with and press 'Go!' to begin the game. Separate morphemes or syllables with dashes (ex. photosynthesis = pho-to-syn-the-sis). You can also play with the default list of words by clicking the 'Default' button. Please limit the length of the words to 14 characters or less.
